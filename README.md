# Retail Sales Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data using **SQL Server** and presents key business insights through an **interactive Excel dashboard**.
The analysis focuses on sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, regional trends, and profitability to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall business performance.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Evaluate regional and customer segment performance.
- Understand the impact of discounts on profitability.
- Build an executive dashboard to visualize key business metrics.

---

## Dataset

- **Dataset:** Sample Superstore
- **Records:** 9,994
- **Customers:** 793
- **Products:** 1,862
- **Orders:** 5,009

## Key Business Insights

- The **West** region generated the highest sales and profit.
- **Technology** was the highest revenue-generating category and also delivered the highest profit.
- The **Consumer** segment contributed approximately half of the company's total sales.
- Monthly sales showed an overall upward trend with seasonal fluctuations.
- Higher discount levels were generally associated with lower average profit, highlighting the impact of excessive discounting on profitability.
